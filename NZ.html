New Zealand

Attractions

Easy walks to the foot of the glacier pass along ancient river valleys with steep sides bearing gigantic horizontal scars from when the glaciers have retreated and advanced over millennia.
Paddle thrilling white-water rapids on the Kaituna River during this rafting excursion from Rotorua. Rafting over Tutea Falls is not for the faint of heart.
Milford Sound cruises must be experienced to be believed. The stunning sheer cliffs surrounding you, the inky blackness of the water and the frosty alpine peaks looming over it all combine to create a stunning tableau.
